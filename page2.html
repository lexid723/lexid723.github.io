About Us

Sharma Institute of Pain Medicine and Rehabilitation, P.A.'s mission is to provide personalized, high-quality care to patients in need of management of both chronic and acute pain conditions. We have created a practice that emphasizes teamwork to better aid our patients in developing and maintaining healthy lifestyles.

We are a pain management practice of dedicated individuals who believe in working with our patients to maintain and improve their health. We work together to serve our patients, treating them and helping to enable them to manage their chronic and acute pain, so as to lead healthier, more satisfying lifestyles. Our physician believes in providing specialized health care services to our patients in a friendly, relaxed atmosphere. We as a practice believe in educating our patients as we work with them to achieve the best physical state possible.
